MACD_Osc_Bullish & MACD_Line_ZeroCross

ADX Trend Breakout

Created by Gerald Appel in 1979, MACD is widely regarded as a masterpiece of trend momentum. To overcome the lagging nature of traditional moving averages, Appel conceived the genius idea of tracking the velocity between two distinct moving averages. When the MACD line crosses above the zero axis or the oscillator turns positive (Bullish), it provides a highly reliable momentum signal confirming that buyers have seized total control of the market's primary direction.
